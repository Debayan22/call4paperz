call4paperz.com

Have you ever had a difficult time to organize your local meetup?

Call4paperz tries to help with event organization, call for papers, voting on speakers and collecting feedback from the participants, and schedule planning.

We have now 11223 users to help with feedback!

Call4paperz was designed, developed and deployed in 48 hours by @anderson_leite, @qmx and @vinibaggio at Rails Rumble 2010.

It's maintained by @ricardovaleriano since 2014.

We are open source. If you have any ideas, bugs or ideas, please visit our GitHub repo. Pull requests and issues are welcome!

If you have any suggestions or questions about Call4Paperz, send us a tweet (@call4paperz).
