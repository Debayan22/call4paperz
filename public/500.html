Have you ever had difficult times to organize your local dev conference?

Call4paperz aims to help with event organization, call for papers, voting on speakers and the basics, like collecting feedback from crowd, schedule planning, etc.

Call4Paperz was designed, developed and deployed in 48 hours by @anderson_leite, @qmx and @vinibaggio at Rails Rumble 2010.

We are strateging some improvements and new features to help every company or user group to keep track of your events.
